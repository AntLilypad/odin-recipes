Cheeseburger

A delicious, juicy cheeseburger

This cheeseburger is so juicy you will never want to eat another cheeseburger for the rest of your life.

Ingredients

  • Frozen burger patty
  • Gouda slice
  • 3 tomato slices
  • Lettuce
  • Burger bun
  • Butter
  • Water
  • Mayonnaise
  • Mustard

Steps

  1. Spread butter on buns and toast them
  2. Put butter into a pan on medium-high heat
  3. Once butter is melted, set burger in the pan and cook for 3 minutes
  4. Flip burger and add a slice of cheese
  5. Add a tablespoon of water to the pan and put a lid on
  6. Cook for another 3 minutes
  7. Add Mayonnaise and Mustard to the bottom bun. Top it with lettuce
  8. Once burger is done cooking, set it on top of the lettuce on the bun
  9. Add tomato slices on top of the burger. Set the top bun on it
