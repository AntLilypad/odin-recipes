Delicious French Fries

These fries are so crispy and delicious, you'll spend the rest of your life judging every other french fry you eat




  • Potatoes
  • Oil
  • Salt
    •

    1. Cut Potatoes into fry shape
    2. Put a bunch of oil into a big pot
    3. Put potatoes into oil
    4. Turn on the stove and heat up the oil
    5. Wait like 20 minutes
    6. Check the color of the fries, they should be yellow
    7. Turn off oil and take fries out
    8. Add salt
